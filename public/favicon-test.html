Testing Instructions

  1. Check the "Current System Theme" section above
  2. Look at your browser tab - it should show the correct favicon
  3. Change your system theme (System Preferences → General → Appearance)
  4. Refresh this page with Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) or Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows/Linux)
  5. The favicon should change to match your system theme

If it's not working:

  • Try opening this page in an incognito/private window
  • Clear your browser cache completely
  • Check if the favicon files are accessible by clicking the preview images above
